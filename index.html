Project Objective: This interactive medical dashboard is designed to analyze key indicators of heart disease. By visualizing data across four distinct charts, users can identify correlations between demographic factors like age and sex against clinical metrics such as cholesterol levels and maximum heart rate, providing an accessible way to interpret complex health datasets.
